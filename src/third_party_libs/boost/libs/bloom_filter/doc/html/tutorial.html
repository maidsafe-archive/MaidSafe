Overview

There are two primitive operations that are supported by all Bloom filters: insert and probably_contains. A third operation, remove, is supported by counting Bloom filters. To effectively use a Bloom filter, the only other concept to understand is the false positive rate.

Bloom filters also support the intersect and union operations. The intersection of two Bloom filters represents the elements that are in both bloom filters. The union of two Bloom filters represents the elements that are in either Bloom filter. Counting bloom filters do not support union and intersection operations.

Types of Bloom Filters Provided

  • Basic Bloom filter (basic_bloom_filter, dynamic_bloom_filter)
  • Counting Bloom filter (counting_bloom_filter, dynamic_counting_bloom_filter)
  • Twohash basic Bloom filter (twohash_basic_bloom_filter, twohash_dynamic_bloom_filter)
  • Twohash counting Bloom filter (twohash_counting_bloom_filter, twohash_dynamic_counting_bloom_filter)
